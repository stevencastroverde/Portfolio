About Me

I have sort of a strange way finding my way into Web Development/Software Engineering....

My name is Steven Castroverde, and I was born and raised in Denver, Colorado. I attended the University of Colorado Boulder and earned my degree in International Relations & Mandarin Chinese. Yes I do speak Chinese and during my undergrad I even lived in China. I love travelling, hiking, skiing, cooking, playing video games, and many more things, but most of all I love coding.

When I got out of college I started working at the Denver County Court as a Judicial Assistant. In this role I was responsible for helping maintain case files, helping judges, and most of all helping the general public. When I was helping people, I started to think of ways technology could help solve their problems, and I saw first hand how technology that never thinks about the user can render software useless. I ended up beginning to teach myself Web Development, and my first project was a HTML email of all things. I became hooked, and I wanted to build larger and more complex apps.
